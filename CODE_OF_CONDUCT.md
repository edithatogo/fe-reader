# Code of Conduct

Fe Reader contributors are expected to keep discussion technical, respectful and focused on making the project safer and more useful.

## Expected Behaviour

- Treat contributors, users and maintainers with respect.
- Assume PDF files, workflows and user reports may contain sensitive information.
- Keep reviews specific to code, contracts, fixtures, evidence and release risk.
- Escalate security, privacy or safety concerns through `SECURITY.md`.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, threats or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private documents, paths, credentials, support bundles or corpus material without permission.
- Pressuring maintainers to bypass policy, review, receipt or release-evidence gates.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide or remove disruptive content, request changes to unsafe contributions, or restrict participation for repeated violations.
