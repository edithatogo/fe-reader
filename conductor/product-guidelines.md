# Product Guidelines

- Use domain-neutral language.
- Treat legal, healthcare, research, government, publishing, education, finance, and engineering as workflow packs.
- Avoid making the product feel like a direct clone of any single commercial app.
- Focus on user trust: reviewable plans, clear warnings, receipts, local-first behaviour.
- Never promise secure redaction unless verification passes.
- Do not prioritise local LLM/RAG UX before deterministic workflows are complete.
