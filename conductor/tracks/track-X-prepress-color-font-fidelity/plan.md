# Track X: Advanced Prepress, Colour & Font Fidelity

## Mission

Support high-fidelity inspection and preservation for publishing, print, engineering and archival PDFs.

## Phases

### X0 Report contract

Deliver `contracts/rust/color_prepress.rs` and `schemas/color-prepress-report.schema.json`.

### X1 Page boxes and output intents

Inspect MediaBox, CropBox, BleedBox, TrimBox, ArtBox and OutputIntents.

### X2 Font diagnostics

Report embedded/subset/missing fonts, ToUnicode presence, CID fonts and extraction risk.

### X3 Colour diagnostics

Detect DeviceCMYK, ICCBased, Separation, DeviceN, spot colours, transparency and overprint indicators.

### X4 Standards bridge

Feed diagnostics into PDF/X, PDF/A and PDF/UA reports.
