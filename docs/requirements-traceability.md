## Maintenance Rules

- Every MoSCoW bullet must have exactly one traceability row.
- Every row must link at least one Conductor track plan, one Mermaid diagram and one evidence artifact.
- `Implemented` rows must point to executable evidence such as a script, contract check, schema validation or crate test target.
- Frontier or optional work stays `Deferred` or `Planned` until feature gates, rollback plans and evidence exist.
- Adding a public surface requires updating this file, `contracts/contract-manifest.yaml`, and the relevant schema or snapshot.
