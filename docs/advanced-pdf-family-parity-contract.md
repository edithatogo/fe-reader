# Advanced PDF Family Parity Contract

Fe Reader meets the advanced PDF family parity contract when the post-launch family matrix and exhaustive registry are both present, linked and validated.
advanced_pdf_baseline

## Contract

- The advanced PDF baseline matrix remains the post-launch claim boundary.
- The exhaustive PDF parity registry remains the canonical taxonomy source.
- Advanced families stay feature-gated and do not block desktop stable launch.
- Families that are only partially supported must remain documented limitations.
- Public claims must continue to point to fixture, contract smoke, visual regression, oracle or limitation evidence.

## Required evidence

- `docs/pdf-baseline-parity-matrix.md`
- `docs/pdf-baseline-parity-matrix.json`
- `docs/pdf-parity-registry.md`
- `docs/pdf-parity-registry.json`
- `scripts/pdf_baseline_parity_check.py`
- `scripts/pdf_parity_registry_check.py`

## Family scope

The contract covers the advanced post-launch PDF families in the registry, including reading, rendering, search, annotations, forms, metadata, redaction, conversion, signatures, attachments/portfolios, OCR, accessibility, repair and standards-related families.

This contract does not claim that every family is fully supported. It binds the advanced family claim boundary to the published taxonomy and nested baseline matrix.
