# Professional Workflow Parity Contract

This professional-workflow parity contract binds forms, redaction and conversion to executable evidence.
professional-workflow-parity

Fe Reader meets the professional workflow parity contract when forms, redaction and conversion remain policy-gated, audit-backed and linked to executable evidence.

## Contract

- Forms workflows remain plan-only until appearance generation, flatten verification, policy review, transaction journaling and audit receipts are wired end to end.
- Secure redaction uses sanitizing rewrite and verification receipts, not incremental append.
- Conversion workflows remain provider-gated and policy-visible.
- Professional workflows remain distinct from the reader baseline and do not broaden stable launch claims.

## Required evidence

- `scripts/forms_contract_smoke.sh`
- `scripts/redaction_verification_smoke.sh`
- `scripts/conversion_contract_smoke.sh`
- `scripts/audit_receipt_contract_check.py`
- `docs/stable-reader-readiness.md`
- `docs/pdf-baseline-parity-matrix.md`
- `docs/pdf-parity-registry.md`

## Coverage

- Forms: fill and flatten are plan-only, with approval and receipt gating.
- Redaction: secure rewrite, verification and human review.
- Conversion: provider discovery and policy-approved output contracts.

This contract does not claim general document editing is fully implemented. It binds the existing professional workflow limits to the repository’s contract and receipt model.
