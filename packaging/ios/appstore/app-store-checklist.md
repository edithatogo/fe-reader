# App Store Checklist

- Archive and sign the iOS/iPadOS app.
- Confirm App Store Connect access, bundle identifier ownership and TestFlight setup.
- Provide App Store privacy manifest and local document-processing disclosures.
- Verify document browser, Files app, share sheet, printing and PencilKit flows.
- Test security-scoped access and sandbox persistence.
- Attach VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, touch target and Pencil alternative accessibility evidence.
- Attach iOS power or Energy Log evidence for open, scroll and annotation flows.
- Prepare TestFlight first and retain review evidence before App Store submission.
- Keep App Intents and share extensions read-only or plan-only unless `patch_plan_id`, `document_hash_match`, `policy_allow_rule`, `approval_token` and audit receipt requirements are satisfied.
